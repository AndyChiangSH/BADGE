TOYOTA THAILAND OPEN 2021 QUARTERFINALS: CAROLINA MARIN CRUISES PAST NESLIHAN YIGIT

Defending champion Carolina Marin showcased her dominance in a straight-set victory against Neslihan Yigit in the quarterfinals of the Toyota Thailand Open 2021.

In the first set, Marin swiftly took control of the game with a series of powerful shots that left Yigit struggling to keep up. Marin capitalized on her opponent's errors, including hitting the net and going out of bounds, to secure a commanding lead and clinch the set 21-6.

The second set saw Yigit putting up more of a fight, with some impressive shots of her own. However, Marin maintained her composure and continued to execute her game plan effectively. Despite a more competitive effort from Yigit, Marin managed to outplay her opponent, taking the set 21-15 and sealing the match in straight sets.

Marin's skillful smash shots and strategic game play were key factors in her victory, as she advanced to the semifinals with a solid performance against Yigit. With this win, Marin showcased why she is considered one of the top contenders in the tournament and will be a force to be reckoned with in the upcoming rounds.
