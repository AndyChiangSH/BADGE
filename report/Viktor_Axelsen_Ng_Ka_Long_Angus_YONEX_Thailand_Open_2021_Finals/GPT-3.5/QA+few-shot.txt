YONEX Thailand Open 2021 Finals: Viktor Axelsen vs Ng Ka Long Angus

In a highly anticipated match, Viktor Axelsen emerged victorious over Ng Ka Long Angus in straight games in the YONEX Thailand Open 2021 Finals.

From the start, Axelsen showcased his dominance on the court, winning the first set with a commanding scoreline of 21-14. The Danish player capitalized on his opponent's errors, with 6 points coming from Angus going out of bounds. Axelsen's powerful smash also proved to be effective, contributing to 6 points in his favor.

As the second set progressed, Axelsen continued his impressive performance, ultimately clinching the victory with a score of 21-14 once again. His strategic use of lob shots caused Angus to lose crucial points, with 5 points slipping away due to this shot type. With a combination of skillful clears and lobs, Axelsen managed to overtake the lead and secure the win at 4-3 in the second set.

Overall, Viktor Axelsen's masterful display of technique and control allowed him to outplay Ng Ka Long Angus in the YONEX Thailand Open 2021 Finals, culminating in a well-deserved victory for the Danish player.
