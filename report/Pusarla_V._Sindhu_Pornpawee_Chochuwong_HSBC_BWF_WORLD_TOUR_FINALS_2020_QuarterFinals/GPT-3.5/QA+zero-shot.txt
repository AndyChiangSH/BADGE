In the HSBC BWF World Tour Finals 2020 Quarterfinals match between Pusarla V. Sindhu and Pornpawee Chochuwong, Pusarla V. Sindhu emerged victorious in two straight sets. In the first set, Pusarla V. Sindhu defeated Pornpawee Chochuwong with a score of 21-18. Pusarla V. Sindhu displayed exceptional skill in landing shots, earning 11 points with precise placement. Despite a brief challenge from Pornpawee Chochuwong, Pusarla V. Sindhu took control of the game with a well-timed smash at 5:4 and sealed the win with a drop shot.

The second set saw Pusarla V. Sindhu continue her dominance, winning with a score of 21-15. Once again, Pusarla V. Sindhu excelled in landing shots, garnering 11 points with expert precision. She utilized a rush shot effectively, earning 3 points to further extend her lead. Although Pornpawee Chochuwong put up a valiant effort, Pusarla V. Sindhu secured the victory with a lob shot.

Overall, Pusarla V. Sindhu's stellar performance in both sets solidified her advancement to the next round of the tournament. Her strong display of skill and strategy ultimately proved too much for Pornpawee Chochuwong to overcome.
