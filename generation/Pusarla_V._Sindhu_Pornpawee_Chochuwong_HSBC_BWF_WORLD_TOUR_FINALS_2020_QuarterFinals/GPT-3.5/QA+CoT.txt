In the HSBC BWF World Tour Finals 2020 QuarterFinals match between Pusarla V. Sindhu and Pornpawee Chochuwong, it was Pusarla V. Sindhu who emerged victorious in both sets. In the first set, Pusarla V. Sindhu won with a score of 21-18, with the majority of her points coming from wins by landing. Her opponent, Pornpawee Chochuwong, struggled to keep up, losing points mainly due to opponent wins by landing.

Pusarla V. Sindhu showcased her skills, earning 4 points with successful drop shots, while Pornpawee Chochuwong lost 3 points by failing to return the shuttlecock over the net. Pusarla V. Sindhu even came from behind to take the lead with a smash at 5-4, eventually winning the set by landing a drop shot.

In the second set, Pusarla V. Sindhu continued her dominance, securing a 21-15 victory over Pornpawee Chochuwong. Once again, Pusarla V. Sindhu capitalized on wins by landing to gain the majority of her points. This time, she scored 3 points with well-executed rushes, while her opponent lost 2 points due to failed clears.

Although there was no comeback in the second set, Pusarla V. Sindhu finished the match in style with a lob shot that sealed her victory. With her impressive performance in both sets, Pusarla V. Sindhu proved to be the stronger player, advancing to the next round of the tournament.
