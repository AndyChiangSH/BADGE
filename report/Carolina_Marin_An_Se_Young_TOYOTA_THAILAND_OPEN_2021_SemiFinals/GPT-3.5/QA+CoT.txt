In an intense SemiFinals match at the TOYOTA THAILAND OPEN 2021, Carolina Marin faced off against An Se Young. The match consisted of two sets, with Carolina Marin emerging victorious in both sets.

In the first set, Carolina Marin secured the win with 21 points, while An Se Young fell short with 19 points. Marin's dominant performance included 9 points from wins by landing, while An Se Young struggled with 9 points lost due to opponent wins by landing. Marin excelled with 4 points earned through net shots, while An Se Young faltered with 4 points lost due to clears. In a comeback victory, Marin used a clear shot to overtake the lead at 7:6 and ultimately ended the game with a rush shot.

Moving on to the second set, Carolina Marin once again emerged victorious with 21 points, while An Se Young finished with 15 points. Marin continued her success with 8 points from wins by landing, while An Se Young struggled with 8 points lost due to opponent wins by landing. Marin capitalized on 3 points from smashing shots, while An Se Young faltered with 7 points lost due to net shots. In a similar fashion to the first set, Marin came from behind to take the lead at 6:5 with a net shot and ended the game with a rush shot victory.

Overall, Carolina Marin's impressive performance secured her a spot in the Finals of the TOYOTA THAILAND OPEN 2021.
