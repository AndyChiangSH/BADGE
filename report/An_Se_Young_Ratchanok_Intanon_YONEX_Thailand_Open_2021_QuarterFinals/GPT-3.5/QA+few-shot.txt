YONEX Thailand Open 2021 QuarterFinals: An Se Young vs. Ratchanok Intanon

In a thrilling quarterfinal clash at the YONEX Thailand Open, An Se Young emerged victorious against Ratchanok Intanon in straight games. 

In the first set, An Se Young showcased her skills and controlled the game with a combination of net shots and pushes. Despite a close fight from Ratchanok Intanon, An Se Young secured the set with a score of 22-20. The young Korean player capitalized on her opponent's mistakes, earning crucial points through errors at the net.

The second set saw An Se Young continue her dominance, with her powerful smashes and well-placed shots putting pressure on Ratchanok Intanon. The Thai player struggled to keep up with An Se Young's relentless play and eventually conceded the set with a score of 21-12. An Se Young once again capitalized on Ratchanok Intanon's mistakes, particularly exploiting weaknesses in her opponent's cross-court net shots.

An Se Young's strategic gameplay and exceptional performance allowed her to advance to the next round, leaving Ratchanok Intanon unable to match her pace and precision on the court.
