In the HSBC BWF World Tour Finals 2020 Quarterfinal match between Pusarla V. Sindhu and Pornpawee Chochuwong, Pusarla V. Sindhu emerged victorious in both sets. In the first set, Sindhu secured a win with 21 points, while Chochuwong obtained 18 points. The most frequent winning reason for Sindhu was landing shots, earning her 11 points, whereas Chochuwong lost 11 points due to opponent winners by landing as well. Sindhu scored the most points with 4 successful drop shots, while Chochuwong lost 3 points with return net errors. Pusarla V. Sindhu managed to come from behind and overtake the lead at 5:4 with a smash shot. She ultimately sealed the win by landing a drop shot.

Moving on to the second set, Pusarla V. Sindhu continued her dominance, winning with 21 points against Chochuwong's 15 points. Once again, the most frequent winning reason for Sindhu was landing shots, resulting in 11 points. Chochuwong, on the other hand, lost 11 points due to opponent winners by landing. Sindhu scored the most points in this set with 3 successful rush shots, while Chochuwong lost 2 points with clear errors. Despite not coming from behind in the second set, Pusarla V. Sindhu secured her victory by landing a lob shot.

Overall, Pusarla V. Sindhu displayed a strong performance in both sets, utilizing her skills effectively to secure a comprehensive win over Pornpawee Chochuwong in the Quarterfinal match of the HSBC BWF World Tour Finals 2020.
