In the YONEX Thailand Open 2021 Finals, Viktor Axelsen faced off against Ng Ka Long Angus in an intense badminton match.

In the first set, Viktor Axelsen emerged victorious with a score of 21-14, showcasing his dominance on the court. Throughout the game, Axelsen capitalized on his opponent's mistakes, earning 6 points as Ng Ka Long Angus frequently went out of bounds. Axelsen's powerful smash also proved to be a key weapon, as he racked up 6 points with this shot. Although Ng Ka Long Angus managed to score with a lob, it wasn't enough to secure a win. Axelsen staged a comeback after trailing briefly at 2-1, using a clear shot to overtake the lead and ultimately winning the set with a well-executed smash.

In the second set, Axelsen once again showed his skill and determination, winning with a score of 21-14. This time, he earned 9 points due to his opponent's out-of-bounds errors and utilized a lob to take the lead at 4-3. The final blow came when Ng Ka Long Angus failed to clear the net with a net shot, giving Axelsen the victory.

Overall, Viktor Axelsen's strategic gameplay and powerful shots secured him the title in the YONEX Thailand Open 2021 Finals, showcasing his prowess on the badminton court.
