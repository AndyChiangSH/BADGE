TOYOTA THAILAND OPEN 2021 QuarterFinals: Carolina Marin vs. Neslihan Yigit

Defending champion Carolina Marin showed her class as she defeated Neslihan Yigit in straight sets in the quarterfinals of the Toyota Thailand Open 2021.

In the first set, Marin dominated the proceedings and showcased her attacking prowess by forcing Yigit into errors and capitalizing on her opponent's mistakes. Marin's accurate shots and aggressive play allowed her to take an early lead and maintain control throughout the set. Yigit tried to fight back but was unable to match Marin's intensity, ultimately losing the set 21-6.

Moving on to the second set, Marin continued her strong performance and displayed her versatility by mixing up her shots and keeping Yigit guessing. Despite a brief resurgence from Yigit in the middle of the set, Marin quickly regained control and closed out the match with a convincing 21-15 victory in the second set.

Marin's clinical display of skill and strategy proved too much for Yigit to handle, as the Spanish star advanced to the semifinals with a straight-set win. With this impressive performance, Marin sent a strong message to her rivals and remains a top contender for the title at the Toyota Thailand Open 2021.
